
# Beyond the Classroom - Homepage Design Plan

## Design Theme
**Purple-Pink-White gradient** with professional, modern aesthetic featuring rich animations, parallax effects, and smooth scroll interactions.

---

## Section 1: Hero Section
- **Full-screen gradient background** (purple to pink to white)
- **Animated text reveal** for the main headline: "Education Beyond the Classroom"
- **Compelling tagline**: "Empowering youth with confidence, skills, and opportunities for life"
- **Two CTA buttons**: "Our Programs" and "Join Us" (linking to external URLs)
- **Floating decorative elements** with subtle parallax movement
- **Scroll indicator animation** at bottom

---

## Section 2: About / Who We Are
- **Split layout**: Text on left, featured event photo on right
- **Key message**: What Beyond the Classroom stands for as a nonprofit
- **SDG badges** (4, 5, 10) with hover effects
- **Fade-in animations** on scroll
- **Quote highlight** from your mission statement

---

## Section 3: The Problem We're Solving
- **Eye-catching statistic**: "80% of Indian graduates are unemployable"
- **Animated counter** for impact numbers
- **Contrasting layout** with bold typography
- **Visual comparison**: "Education produces degree-holders. The future needs skill-holders."
- **Dark gradient section** for emphasis

---

## Section 4: Vision & Mission
- **Two beautiful cards** side by side
- **Vision card** with inspiring future-focused message
- **Mission card** explaining two pillars: On-Ground Impact + Leadership Development
- **Subtle gradient borders** and hover lift effects
- **Icon animations** on scroll

---

## Section 5: The 3B Model (Build • Bridge • Become)
- **Interactive 3-step visual** with animated connectors
- **Each step** as a card with icon, title, and description
- **Staggered entrance animations**
- **Gradient accent colors** for each step
- **Background pattern/texture** for visual interest

---

## Section 6: Our Programs
- **Tab/toggle interface** switching between "Current Programs" and "Upcoming Programs"
- **Current**: Financial Literacy, Digital Literacy, Civic Awareness, Women's Health
- **Upcoming**: Youth Summit, Podcast, Youth Dialogues, Fellowships
- **Cards with icons** and brief descriptions
- **Workshop photos** integrated as backgrounds

---

## Section 7: Impact & Recognition
- **Animated number counters**: 1500+ students, 30+ partnerships
- **Partner logos/names** in a scrolling marquee or grid
- **Recognition badges** (Ministries, NITI Aayog, AICTE, etc.)
- **Photo collage** from events using your uploaded images
- **Testimonial or achievement highlight**

---

## Section 8: Join Our Team
- **Gradient highlight section** for volunteer recruitment
- **Open positions**: Graphic Designer, Video Editor, Social Media Lead
- **What you'll get** and **Why apply** highlights
- **Bold CTA button** linking to your application form
- **Dynamic background with subtle patterns**

---

## Section 9: Collaborate With Us
- **Partnership invitation section**
- **Ways to collaborate**: Host programs, Partner on initiatives, Funding, Mentorship
- **Contact email** prominently displayed
- **Social media links** with hover animations
- **Inspiring closing message**

---

## Footer
- **Gradient footer** matching theme
- **Quick links** to sections
- **Social media icons**
- **Newsletter email mention** or contact info
- **Copyright and nonprofit tagline**

---

## Animations & Interactions
- Parallax scrolling effects on hero and key sections
- Smooth scroll-triggered fade-in animations
- Animated number counters for statistics
- Hover effects on cards, buttons, and images
- Subtle floating elements and decorative shapes
- Smooth section transitions

---

## Images Integration
Your uploaded photos will be used as:
- Hero section background accent
- About section feature image
- Programs section visual backgrounds
- Impact section photo gallery/collage
